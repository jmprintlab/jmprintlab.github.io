The workshop

Made by one person, in one small room.

JM Print Lab started with small fixes around the house — a broken hook, a missing knob — and turned into a habit of printing things worth keeping.

Everything is printed on one Bambu Lab A1, queued up, watched over, and packed by hand. No factories, no big team. Just a printer, some filament, and a lot of ideas.

The machine

Why a Bambu Lab A1?

It's reliable, it's accurate, and it can run for hours without much fuss — which matters when you're printing parts that need to click, snap, and fit together properly.

It also prints in multiple colors, which is handy for fidgets that look as good as they feel.

How it gets made

From idea to your doorstep.

Every order, stock or custom, goes through the same four steps before it ships.

Design

The part gets modeled and test-printed, to make sure it fits together and moves the way it should.

Slice

Settings get tuned for the object — fidgets get smoother walls, sturdier parts get more infill.

Print

It's printed and watched on the Bambu Lab A1, then cleaned up by hand once it's done.

Pack

Every part gets handled and tested before it's boxed up — if it doesn't feel right, it gets reprinted.
